“We’ve been working with Christopher since 2016 and have loved seeing him grow as an artist. Our collaboration began after we ordered a series of nudes from his print shop, just as he was on the cusp of an international breakthrough. It’s rare to find an artist with such a broad range of what they can do, and Chris does it well. His aesthetic has attracted countless commissions from brands as diverse as Tiffany & Co., Twitter, and New Balance, to name a few.

Chris’s debut NFT collection is serene, restful, and optimistic — in this saturated digital world, the unique artworks of The Garden stand out. Chris began creating his ‘Flortraits’ to rest and feel creative. We’re fascinated by the outcome. He has always excelled in both digital and physical forms of expression; therefore, expanding the collection from NFTs to limited-edition prints feels like a natural progression.”

— Pablo Steffa, co-founder & creative director at Fount Gallery
